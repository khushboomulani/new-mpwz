  2. Ensure healthy earthing be installed at the premises.
  3. Wiring done through licensed electrical contractors only.
  4. Use ISI marked equipments and cables of proper capacity in the installation.
  5. Use proper size fuse wire in main switches.
  6. Provide weather proof enclosures to meter and metering equipments. Safe guard the mater & other equipment of the MPPKVVCL in the premises.
  7. Replace promptly defective electrical fittings and appliances.
  8. Donâ€™t use bare/hanging wires extending supply from are point to other or from one premises to other premises. It is hazardous and causes accidents.
  9. Avoid loose connection and points.
  10. Do not meddles with live wire/points
  11. Do not plant trees below the overhead power lines.
  12. Do not construct your building underneath HT or LT line without proper clearance.
  13. Avoid theft of energy.
  14. Always call licensed electrical contractor for rectification of fault in your premises.
  15. For any difficulty related to electrical supply, always contact MPPKVVCL authorities at their offices.
  16. Donâ€™t touch electric switch/appliance if your hands are wet or bleeding from a cut.
  17. Donâ€™t keep the lamp holder without lamp.

If the power goes off.---

  1. Confirm the outage is in your home only, most likely a fuse has blown or a circuit breaker has opened. This could be a warning of overloaded wiring or a defective appliances check the neighborhood to determine how widespread the outage is.
  2. Report the outage at fuse off call centre or distt. Centre.
  3. Turn off all appliances that come on automatically.To prevent over-loading of electrical circuit at the time of restoring power, shut off appliances like water heater, refrigerator water pump and air-conditioner.
  4. Similarly to avoid damage from lightning or power surge disconnect all sensitive appliance viz TVs computers VCRs/VCP Micro oven etc.
  5. Turn off all appliances which were operated.
  6. Always keep on alternate source of light on hand such as flash light emergency lights candles with matches, oil lamp with sufficient fuel.
  7. If a stand by generator is installed, ensure it is installed and wired properly with adouble throw switch.This will prevent electricity from flowing to the outside lines.

When power comes back â€“

  1. Turn on only most essential appliance when power first comes back wait 10-15 minutes before reconnecting the other appliances.
  2. Before witching back to normal heat/cool setting, check the outdoor units to assure it is free from brush or leaves
